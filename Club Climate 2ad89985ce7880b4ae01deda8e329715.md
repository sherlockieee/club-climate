**Mission:** We are creating **tight-knit community** of **climate-interested early professionals** who are looking to build climate knowledge + get honest guidance on personal and professional growth

</aside>

Founded in 2023, our group is based in New York and meets twice a month to discuss books and other content focused on climate change and energy transition. What started as a casual meeting of friends of friends from all walks of life turned into a tight-knit community of young professionals who share their knowledge and work experiences at the forefront of business, finance, technology and science

As of November 2025, our group has ~30 active members, from 15+ countries
